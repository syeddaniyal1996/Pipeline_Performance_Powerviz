# Pipeline Performance Risk Analytics Powerviz
To identify conversion trends and regional growth opportunities while providing deep-dive insights into funnel attrition and at-risk deals.

I’m thrilled to share a deep dive into our latest Strategic Growth & Pipeline Analytics project! By leveraging the advanced capabilities of PowerViz visuals, we’ve transformed complex data into a clear, actionable narrative to bridge our target gaps.

# 🔍 Key Insights from the Dashboard:
Strategic Growth & Pipeline: We are currently tracking a Total Pipeline of €1.02bn with a weighted value of €429M. While we’ve seen a massive 291.4% increase vs LY, we are focused on closing the €70.9M gap to target. 

# Regional Performance: 
Our Weighted Pipeline is distributed across key regions, with the Netherlands (€39.05M) and Spain (€35.08M) leading the way. 


# Operational Efficiency: 
We’re seeing a solid 23.5% conversion rate and an average of 131 days to close, helping us refine our tactical deep-dives. 


# Risk Mitigation: 
Our Risk Forecast (2022-2026) and At-Risk Deal Network allow us to pinpoint exactly which stages—like Negotiation and Contracting—require immediate intervention. 

# 🛠️ Visual Highlights:
This project wouldn't have the same impact without the incredible PowerViz library.  We utilized:


Sankey Charts for Pipeline Distribution by Product and Stage. 
Sunburst & Donut Charts for geographic distribution. 
Advanced Funnel & Activity Distribution to correlate sales effort to deal success. 
Horizon Charts for multi-year risk forecasting.
